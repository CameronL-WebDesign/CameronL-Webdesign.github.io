Love Cafe serves up comfort food and connects in Muskegon

Muskegon's New Cafe

A new pay-what-you-can restaurant has become a hit for anyone who walks by and needs a little help with food.

The cafe features a model that empowers diners to "pay it forward" by contributing what they can afford after each meal. This allows everyone, from donors to neighbors in need, to dine together in a spirit of generosity and belonging.

Mike Miller, executive director of Love INC of Muskegon, said the organization's mission is to empower locals with the dignity of choice.

We're so excited for the community coming around and visiting us,Miller said. It's a limited menu, but everything is from scratch. All high-end dishes, they all come out Instagram ready -- very prettily plated.
