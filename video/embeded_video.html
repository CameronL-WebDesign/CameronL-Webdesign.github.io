How to Embed a YouTube Video Into a Website

This tutorial explains how to embed a service-hosted video into a webpage using YouTube.

Step 1: Open the YouTube Video

Go to YouTube and open the video you want to embed. Make sure the video allows embedding.

Step 2: Click the Share Button

Under the video player, click the Share button.

Step 3: Choose Embed

In the sharing menu, click the Embed option.

Step 4: Copy the Embed Code

YouTube will display HTML embed code. Click Copy.

Step 5: Paste the Code Into Your HTML File

Open your HTML file and paste the embed code where you want the video to appear.
