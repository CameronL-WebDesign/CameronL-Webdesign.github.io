Global Exotic Fuits Overview
Fruit Name Primary Origin Taste Profile
Dragon Fruit Central & South America Mildly sweet; a cross between a kiwi and a pear.

Mangosteen Southeast Asia A perfect balance of sweet and tangy; often called the "Queen of Fruits."
