Global Exotic Fruits Overview

  • Dragon Fruit is found in Central and South America. It is mildly sweet, a cross between a kiwi and a pear.
  • Durian is found in Southeast Asia. It is rich and custard-like, though notoriously pungent, which causes it to be banned in some parts of Asia.
  • Mangosteen is found in Southeast Asia. It is a perfect balance of sweet and tangy, often called the "Queen of Fruits."
  • Rambutan is found in Indonesia and Malaysia. It is sweet and juicy, similar to a grape with a floral hint.
